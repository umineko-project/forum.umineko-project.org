Basically the issue is that we do not have Android devices at all. I can debug on iOS, but not on Android. There is also a bigger issue of different Android devices behaving differently. And they are not just behaving differently, they have various bugs and glitches, like the one you have, which need unintuitive hacks and workarounds we cannot guess prior to exploring them.

Long story short, to support your device we would have had to get it and find a fair amount of time to explore the issue. That means that the fix is unlikely happening from our side. On the other side, the engine is open-source (https://github.com/umineko-project/onscripter-ru), and as long as you provide a patch, the problem may be resolved.
