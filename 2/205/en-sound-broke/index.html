We had a discussion on this topic, and agreed to change some less common keyboard bindings. Here is the list of changed ones:
- shift+q (quit) — removed, use standard cmd+q (alt+f4)
- m (mute) — changed to opt+m (alt+m)
- s (skip) — changed to opt+s (alt+s)
- e (screenshot) — changed to opt+e (alt+e)
- DualShock HOME — bound to screenshot
