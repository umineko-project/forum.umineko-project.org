2021-01-07 11:28:36

They are under graphics/system/wnd. Note that you'll have to tweak the files somewhat to get them to appear as they do in-game, as the engine resizes and repositions certain parts of the textbox dynamically to fit names and text in. You could also grab the pre-rendered textboxes from 07th-Mod, here, but do note that they look somewhat different from those of Umineko Project (in particular due to a different font), and they also lack the Type-B textbox.
