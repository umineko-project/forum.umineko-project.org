2b. After "Enjoy this game that I've created...just for you......," enter the menu, click "Re-read story." Choose any twilight. Save file. Load the file. Then (almost) the whole logs of "Bern's Puzzle" will be erased except for the First Twilight plus any other Twilight you have chosen. Once that happens, everytime you open the logs, you will be located at the beginning of EP8.

The same applies when you click "Hints." Then click any hint to open it. Save file. Load the file. Then (almost) the whole logs of "Bern's Puzzle" will be erased except for the First Twilight plus any hint you have chosen.

For example, this is the normal, correct logs that shows when you click "Hints" and click "??????????????" but do not load the file you saved at this very point. Open the logs, and you can see the hint's text follows right after the last line of the chapter:
Umineko no Naku Koro ni Chiru ~Nocturne of Truth and Illusions~ (8.2a) 6_1_2020 11_28_51 PM.png (1.96 MiB) Viewed 22444 times
But if you load the saved file, and open the logs, you will be located at the beginning of EP8. Scrolling down, you can only see the hint's text follows right after the First Twilight text's end instead. All other things in the chapter (except the First Twilight text and this hint's text) are gone.
Umineko no Naku Koro ni Chiru ~Nocturne of Truth and Illusions~ (8.2a) 6_1_2020 11_30_16 PM.png (1.92 MiB) Viewed 22444 times
One more observation: once most of the logs of "Bern's Puzzle" were wiped out, you can't restore them simply by reloading or jumping to an earlier point within that chapter. Rather, you have to reload a file that has saved a point preceding "Bern's Puzzle" (that is, before the opening of the First Twilight"), and then you will see all the gone contents are restored back to the logs.
