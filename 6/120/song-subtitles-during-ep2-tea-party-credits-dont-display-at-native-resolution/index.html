This one is an unfortunate optimization. We use software rendering for subtitles, which works well when rendering a video (OPs etc have 1080p subs), but unfortunately, it means that sub performance is actually lower when subtitles are used with the rest of the game's hardware-accelerated textures. We had to reduce the resolution to get acceptable performance without the video.

If we can think of a way to fix it, we certainly will.
